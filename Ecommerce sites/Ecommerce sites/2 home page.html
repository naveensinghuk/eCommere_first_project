  2. eBay.










    eBay is an American eCommerce company that was established in 1995. They are the world’s leading consumer-to-consumer marketplace as well as one of the world’s leaders in business-to-consumer retail.

    eBay delivers to about 190 countries and territories around the world. They also have about 138 million buyers around the world.

    Buyers don’t need to pay any transaction fees on eBay. However, it is quite different for sellers as they have to pay for listing items on the eCommerce platform.

    Features

    Listing Items

    You sell on eBay by simply registering on the platform and simply listing items. The first 250 listings are free, and after that, there’s a non-refundable insertion fee of $0.35 per listing.

    Buy-It-Now

    eBay started as an auction platform. In addition to the auction feature, they added the Buy-It-Now feature, which allows sellers to fix a price listing their items. Sellers can also set the Buy It Now price on their auction-style listings.

    The price is set higher than the auction price and allows the buyer to purchase the items right away.

    eBay Advanced Search

    The eBay advanced search allows buyers to narrow their search to search range, number of bids, price range, and currency of the sale.
